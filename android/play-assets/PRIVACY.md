# Privacy Policy — openblocks

_Last updated: July 4, 2026_

openblocks ("the app") is a single-player falling-block puzzle game. This policy
explains, plainly, what the app does with your information: **nothing.**

## Data we collect

**None.** openblocks does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
information or usage data. Specifically:

- The app requests **no Android permissions** and has **no internet access** —
  it cannot send anything off your device.
- There are **no accounts, logins, or sign-ins.**
- There is **no analytics, telemetry, advertising, or third-party SDKs.**
- Your game state (scores, settings) stays on your device and is never
  transmitted.

Because the app cannot access the network, no data ever leaves your device.

## Children

openblocks is suitable for all ages. Since it collects no data from anyone, it
collects no data from children.

## Third parties

openblocks contains no third-party services, ad networks, or trackers, and does
not share data with anyone (there is no data to share).

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with
a revised "Last updated" date.
